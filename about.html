Our Story

Mom'z Factory was born out of a simple belief — that what you put on your skin matters as much as what you put in your body. We set out to create handmade soaps that are honest, effective, and kind to the earth.

Every bar is crafted by hand using natural ingredients sourced carefully, with no harsh chemicals or synthetic additives. From our home in Karumathampatti, Coimbatore, we deliver clean, green, organic goodness straight to your door.
